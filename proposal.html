3.2 Compliance (Switzerland / EU)

  • Human-in-the-loop default — no message sends without explicit approval
  • No LinkedIn scraping — platform ToS and nDSG/GDPR exposure
  • Enrichment scope — CSV fields + optional public website text only
  • Full audit trail — exportable log per contact and campaign
  • Data residency — deployed on infrastructure you control

3.3 Why this architecture

Separating the approval UI (Next.js), business logic (FastAPI), and automation layer (n8n) means your team can maintain workflows visually while developers extend enrichment and AI prompts without touching send infrastructure.
